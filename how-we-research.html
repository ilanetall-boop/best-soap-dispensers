How We Research

At Best Soap Dispensers, we're committed to providing accurate, unbiased, and helpful product recommendations. Here's an inside look at our research process.

Our 5-Step Process

1. Market Analysis

We start by surveying the entire soap dispenser market on Amazon, identifying the top-rated products across different categories: manual pump, automatic touchless, wall-mounted, foaming, and refillable designs.

2. Customer Review Deep-Dive

For each product, we analyze hundreds of verified customer reviews to identify real-world performance patterns — not just the star rating, but the specific praise and complaints from actual buyers.

3. Specification Comparison

We compare technical specs side-by-side: material (stainless steel, glass, ceramic, plastic), capacity (oz/ml), pump type, sensor range, battery life, and refill compatibility.

4. Value Assessment

We evaluate the price-to-quality ratio. A $12 dispenser that outperforms a $40 one deserves recognition. We highlight the best value picks in every category.

5. Editorial Review

Every article goes through our editorial quality check. We verify all claims, ensure accurate pricing, and confirm Amazon availability before publishing.

Our Standards

We only feature products with 4+ star ratings and 500+ verified reviews. We never accept payment for product placement. Our recommendations are based solely on merit.

Continuous Updates

Products change. Prices fluctuate. New options emerge. We regularly update our articles to reflect the latest market conditions and customer feedback.
