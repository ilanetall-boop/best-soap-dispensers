My Story

With over 10 years of experience in interior design and home products, I've made it my mission to help homeowners make informed decisions about their bathroom and kitchen essentials.

After working as a buyer for major home goods retailers, I noticed how overwhelming the soap dispenser market had become. Hundreds of options — from basic pump bottles to smart touchless systems — with no reliable comparison data.

That's why I created Best Soap Dispensers — to provide honest, data-driven reviews based on real research, not just manufacturer specs.

Every product on this site has been carefully evaluated using our rigorous 5-point methodology.
